## Slide 3 — The Problem

Malaysian micro-SMEs run the country's everyday economy — on **WhatsApp threads, paper logbooks, and Google Sheets**.

| Why existing tools fail them | |
|------------------------------|---|
| **SAP / Odoo** | Too expensive. Shaped for Sdn Bhd, not Enterprise sole props. |
| **Xero / QuickBooks** | Finance only. No operations, HR, sales motion. |
| **StoreHub / Loyverse** | POS only. No CRM, no LHDN, no AI. |
| **WhatsApp + Excel** | Zero structure. Knowledge lives in one phone. |

The result: a ~RM 5K/month kedai owner has **no software fit** between RM 0 (free spreadsheets) and RM 300+/month (enterprise tools they can't justify).

> [Speaker notes]
> Find the persona in the room and address them: "If you've ever helped a family member running a kedai or kafe — this is them."

## Slide 5 — Product Hero: The Executive Boardroom

**One business question. A virtual exec committee. Sub-cent cost.**

```
User: "I want to offer a Buy-1-Free-1 deal to clear our beauty stocks."

[Marketing AI]  → 40% target interest. Good strategy.
[Finance AI]    → Warning: margin drops 60% → 20%. Breakeven at 200 units.
[Operations AI] → 250 expiring items. 3 days packing.
[HR AI]         → Part-time packing support: RM240.

→ Synthesized: Cap promo at 250 units. 5-day window. RM240 budget. Go.
```

- Unlocks when ≥ 2 AI Agents are subscribed (RM15–20/mo each).
- Cost to us per run: **~RM 0.005** on GPT-4o-mini.
- Revenue per run: **~RM 0.80** at top-up rates.
- Gross margin per Boardroom run: **>99%**.

> [Speaker notes]
> This is the moat. A solo owner gets what costs a SME RM 50K/month in salaried executives. For RM 50/month.

## Slide 12 — Competitive Moat

Three reinforcing layers — none of which can be copied with a price match.

1. **Cross-pillar data flywheel.** Each pillar a customer activates makes the others sharper. Customer adds HR → payroll auto-posts to Finance → AI's P&L gets smarter → Boardroom gets better. Switching cost compounds monthly.

2. **AI economics moat.** Structured triggers + GPT-4o-mini + Slow Mode = the **only** architecture that ships enterprise-grade AI at micro-SME pricing **without burning cash**. Competitors will either skip AI (boring product) or burn capital (broken margin) trying to match us.

3. **Malaysia-native compliance.** LHDN XML schema mapping, DuitNow rails, BM copy, IC handling, AL/EL/MC. Global tools won't bother. Local tools won't fund AI. We sit in the gap.

> [Speaker notes]
> "We win because no single competitor can simultaneously cover six pillars, ship Boardroom AI at this margin, and be Malaysia-native. Each one is hard; together they're a category."
