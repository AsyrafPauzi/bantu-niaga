# Pricing Plan

> Pricing is designed for a natural growth path: start with money discipline, then add business structure, staff and sales control, and finally customer retention.

---

## 1. Pricing Strategy

Bantu Niaga should not feel cheap, but it also cannot feel like an expensive ERP.

The pricing model follows this rule:

- **Plans unlock modules and operating limits.**
- **Add-ons unlock premium capabilities inside modules.**
- **AI is an optional premium layer, not required for core workflows.**

Commercial caveat: before public launch, verify current Bukku pricing and keep Bantu Niaga's paid entry plan above a finance-only baseline while still affordable for Malaysian micro SMEs. Bantu Niaga can justify the premium because Starter includes Finance, Admin, and Operations rather than finance alone.

This creates a clean upgrade path:

- **Free:** start tracking invoices and payments.
- **Starter:** organize the business.
- **Growth:** manage staff and sales.
- **Pro:** grow customers and marketing.

## 2. Base Plans

### Free — RM0/month

For solo entrepreneurs who need basic invoice and payment discipline.

Included module:

- Finance.

Included limits:

- 1 owner seat.
- No saved customer database.
- 5 GB storage.
- No add-on purchases.

Best for:

- Freelancers.
- New home sellers.
- Solo service providers.
- Owners who mainly need invoices, income tracking, receipt upload for records, and payment tracking.

Core value:

- Track income.
- Create and share invoices.
- Upload receipts.
- Track payment status.
- Send WhatsApp-ready payment reminders.

Upgrade trigger:

- The owner starts needing expense tracking, saved customers, document storage, task tracking, products, suppliers, orders, bookings, or add-ons.

## 3. Add-on Pricing Principles

Add-ons require a paid plan. The Free plan cannot activate add-ons.

Paid plans can only activate add-ons for modules included in the current plan. For example, Starter can buy Finance, Admin, and Operations add-ons, but not Sales, HR, or Marketing add-ons.

Add-ons should be priced when they do one or more of these:

- Save repeated work through automation.
- Add advanced reporting or forecasting.
- Connect to outside platforms.
- Add approval, audit, or compliance controls.
- Support more staff, stock, locations, or volume.
- Consume AI credits, storage, messaging, or paid infrastructure.

Suggested add-on price bands:

- Small utility add-ons: **RM8-RM15/month**.
- Workflow add-ons: **RM15-RM35/month**.
- Integration add-ons: **RM25-RM60/month**.
- AI agents: **RM15-RM20/month per module agent**.
- One-time credit packs: **RM10-RM50** depending on credit amount.

Examples:

- Extra 10 GB Storage: RM8/month.
- Extra Staff Seat: RM15/seat/month.
- Dynamic DuitNow QR: RM15-RM25/month.
- WhatsApp Business API: RM35/month before usage pass-through.
- LHDN E-Invoice Connector: RM35-RM60/month or included in selected higher bundles later.
- Per-Module AI Agent: RM15-RM20/month.

## 5. Discount and Trial Guidance

Free plan:

- No trial needed because it is permanently free.
- No add-on purchase.
- No saved customer database.
- No expense tracking.

Paid plans:

- Offer a **14-day trial** for Starter, Growth, and Pro.
- Do not require a credit card for early beta; require one later when conversion is proven.

Annual payment:

- Offer **2 months free** for annual prepay.
- Example: pay 10 months, get 12 months.

Founder/early customer discount:

- Time-box it clearly.
- Prefer "first 100 businesses keep this price for 12 months" instead of lifetime discounts.

Multi-business discount:

- Consider **10%-20% off** for owners with 2 or more businesses.
- Keep billing per business so data, staff, and modules stay cleanly separated.

## 6. Pricing Positioning

Customer-facing plan names should be simple:

- **Free:** Start.
- **Starter:** Organize.
- **Growth:** Manage.
- **Pro:** Grow.

Internal explanation:

- Free captures solo entrepreneurs early without giving away expense tracking, customer database, or add-ons.
- Starter converts micro SMEs that feel operational pain.
- Growth monetizes staff and sales complexity.
- Pro monetizes customer retention and marketing maturity.

The pricing story should always connect price to business maturity, not just feature quantity.
