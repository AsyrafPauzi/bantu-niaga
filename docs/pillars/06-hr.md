# Module 6 — HR

> Keep staff records safe and leave organized.

## 1. Purpose

HR helps owners stop storing sensitive staff information in chat threads. Core should cover employee records and basic leave management. Payroll, statutory submissions, rostering, time clock, advanced policies, and HR automation belong in add-ons.

## 2. Target Users

- Solo entrepreneurs preparing to hire their first helper.
- Micro SMEs with a few employees and basic leave tracking needs.
- Growing SMEs that later need scheduling, payroll support, statutory workflows, and stronger reminders.

## 3. Core Features

Core HR should cover staff records and basic leave:

- Employee profile.
- IC/passport document storage.
- Emergency contact record.
- Bank account record.
- Employment type and role.
- Staff document folder.
- Basic leave tracker.
- Annual leave, emergency leave, and MC records.
- Leave approval.
- Public holiday reference calendar.
- Onboarding checklist.

## 4. Add-on Features

HR add-ons should carry policy depth, scheduling, payroll, and automation:

- Advanced Leave Policy.
- Contract and Letter Generator.
- HR Reminder Pack.
- Self-Service Leave Forms.
- Shift Roster.
- Time Clock.
- Payroll Estimate.
- Payroll Bank Export.
- Statutory Payroll Pack.
- EA Form Generator.
- HR AI Assistant.

## 5. Key User Flows

### 5.1 Add an employee

1. Owner creates employee profile.
2. Owner enters role, employment type, start date, emergency contact, and bank details.
3. Owner uploads IC/passport and related staff documents.
4. Employee appears in the staff list.

### 5.2 Record leave

1. Owner opens the leave tracker.
2. Owner chooses employee, leave type, date range, and reason.
3. Owner approves or rejects the leave.
4. Leave balance and calendar are updated.

### 5.3 Complete onboarding

1. Owner applies the onboarding checklist.
2. Owner marks items such as IC collected, bank details collected, uniform given, and SOP briefing done.
3. Outstanding onboarding items remain visible until completed.

## 6. Data Notes

Core tables should cover employees, sensitive document links, emergency contacts, bank details, leave records, leave statuses, public holiday references, and onboarding checklist items. Sensitive fields must be protected by role-based access and private storage.

Premium add-ons can extend the model with carry-forward rules, custom policies, self-service links, shift records, time clock events, payroll export batches, statutory contribution records, EA form outputs, HR reminders, and AI-generated HR drafts.
