# Target Market and Plan Fit

> Bantu Niaga is for owner-led Malaysian businesses that need structure without ERP complexity.

---

## 1. Target Market

Bantu Niaga focuses on three customer groups.

### Solo Entrepreneurs

One-person businesses that need speed, simple money discipline, and less mental load.

Typical users:

- Freelancers.
- Home sellers.
- Home bakers.
- Small service providers.
- Agents.
- Owner-operators with no staff yet.

Main pain:

- Income and payment records are scattered.
- Invoices and payment follow-up are manual.
- Receipts live in phone gallery or WhatsApp.
- The owner wants a simple system, not accounting software.

Best entry plan:

- **Free** when they mainly need income, invoices, and payment tracking.
- **Starter** when they also need expenses, customers, documents, tasks, orders, suppliers, or bookings.

### Micro SMEs

Small teams that need structure for daily operations.

Typical users:

- Kedai.
- Small cafes.
- Salons.
- Tuition operators.
- Homestay operators.
- Car wash or detailing shops.
- Small contractors.

Main pain:

- Staff and owner tasks are unclear.
- Products, services, suppliers, and orders are not organized.
- Customer and payment records are split across WhatsApp, Excel, and notebooks.
- The business is not large, but it is already too busy to run manually.

Best entry plan:

- **Starter** for admin and operations structure.
- **Growth** when the business has staff, counter sales, leads, POS, or HR needs.

### Growing SMEs

Owner-led SMEs with staff, sales activity, customer retention needs, and compliance pressure.

Typical users:

- Multi-staff cafes.
- Active online sellers.
- Growing salons.
- Service teams.
- Small retail chains.
- Businesses with repeat customers and campaigns.

Main pain:

- The owner needs roles, controls, reporting, and better customer follow-up.
- Marketing and sales should connect to real revenue.
- Staff records and leave need to be managed properly.
- The business wants advanced features, but not full ERP complexity.

Best entry plan:

- **Growth** for staff and sales control.
- **Pro** when customer CRM, segments, content planning, coupons, and retention workflows matter.

## 4. Simple Sales Positioning

- **Free:** start tracking invoices and payments.
- **Starter:** organize the business.
- **Growth:** manage staff and sales.
- **Pro:** grow customers and marketing.

The upgrade path should feel natural: as the business adds documents, operations, staff, sales, and marketing pressure, the next plan becomes easier to justify.
